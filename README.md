# 📉 Customer Retention Intelligence Platform

An AI-powered Customer Churn Prediction and Retention Intelligence System that helps businesses identify high-risk customers, estimate revenue impact, calculate customer lifetime value, and generate actionable retention strategies.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses, SaaS companies, telecom operators, banks, and e-commerce platforms.

This platform uses customer behavior metrics to:

- Predict churn probability
- Calculate customer health score
- Estimate customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Measure potential revenue loss
- Recommend retention actions

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎯 Churn Prediction Engine

Predicts the likelihood of a customer leaving.

Inputs:

- Customer Tenure
- Monthly Charges
- Support Tickets
- Satisfaction Score
- Contract Type

Output:

Churn Probability: 78%
Risk Level: High Risk

---

### ❤️ Customer Health Score

Calculates overall customer health.

Output:

Health Score: 82/100

Higher score = healthier customer relationship.

---

### 💰 Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

Estimates customer value over their relationship with the company.

Formula:

CLV = Monthly Charges × Tenure

Example:

CLV = ₹1,20,000

---

### 📉 Revenue Impact Analysis

Calculates potential annual revenue loss due to churn.

Example:

Potential Revenue Loss
₹48,000

---

### ⚠️ Risk Classification

Automatically categorizes customers into:

| Churn Probability | Risk Level |
|------------------|-------------|
| 0–24% | 🟢 Low Risk |
| 25–49% | 🟡 Medium Risk |
| 50–74% | 🟠 High Risk |
| 75–100% | 🔴 Critical Risk |

---

### 📊 Customer Intelligence Dashboard

Interactive analytics dashboard using Plotly.

Metrics:

- Customer Health Score

- Satisfaction Score

---

### 🎯 Retention Recommendation Engine

Provides actionable retention strategies.

Examples:

### 📈 Business Impact Analysis

Helps management understand:

### 📥 Downloadable Reports

Generate downloadable customer retention reports.
